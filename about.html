what it is

A model-training workbench, built for the browser.

TrainSpace adapts a pre-trained network to recognize the images, sounds, and poses you feed it — then hands the result straight to your own sites, apps, and connected hardware.

01

Gather

Group your examples into classes — the categories you want the model to tell apart.

02

Train

Train the model, then test it live to see how well it separates your classes.

03

Export

Download the trained model or host it directly — ready for sites, apps, and IoT devices.

under the hood

Transfer learning, running entirely on your device.

Built on TensorFlow.js, the model trains and predicts locally — no webcam frame or sample ever leaves your browser.
