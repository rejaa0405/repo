Frequently asked

q1 Who is this for?

Educators, makers, students, and builders of every kind — anyone with an idea worth exploring. No prior machine learning experience is required.

q2 How does it actually work?

You feed the system examples — images, audio, or poses — for each category. Transfer learning adapts a pre-trained network to your data, right there in your browser tab.

q3 Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. The dashboard handles training end to end. If you do write code, export the trained model into your own web, app, or hardware project.

q4 What happens to my data?

It stays on your device. Training runs locally — your webcam feed, microphone audio, and images are never uploaded to a server.

q5 How does the IoT connection work?

When a prediction crosses a confidence threshold, the dashboard sends the detected class to your endpoint, so a physical device can react in near real time.
